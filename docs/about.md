# About Us

Group Mini is a platform for managing savings groups — sometimes called SACCOs, chamas, or rotating savings clubs.

## What We Do

Group Mini makes it easy for groups of people to pool money, track contributions, manage payouts, and stay organized — all in one place.

Key capabilities:

- **Group management** — Create and manage groups with custom rules, contribution amounts, and schedules
- **Member invitations** — Invite members and manage roles within your group
- **Account tracking** — Monitor group accounts, balances, and financial activity
- **Multi-currency support** — Work with local currencies including UGX

## Our Mission

To make informal financial cooperation accessible, transparent, and trustworthy for communities everywhere.
