# Authentication

This guide covers how to access the Group Mini application, create an account, and verify your email.

## Logging In

To log in to your account:
1. Navigate to the login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click "Sign In".

![Login Page](/images/login.png)

## Creating an Account

If you don't have an account:
1. Click on the "Sign Up" or "Join" link.
2. Fill in the required details (Email, Password).
3. Click "Create Account".

![Signup Page](/images/signup.png)

## Verifying Your Email

After creating an account, you may need to verify your email.
If your email is unconfirmed, you will see a message indicating that you need to verify it.

![Email Unconfirmed](/images/email_unconfirmed.png)

Follow the instructions in the email sent to you to complete the verification.
