# Authentication

This guide covers how to access the Group Mini application, create an account, and manage your credentials.

## Logging In

To log in to your account:
1. Navigate to the login page at `https://group-mini.smartxuls.club/login`.
2. Enter your Email or Phone number.
3. Enter your Password.
4. Click "Sign in".

You can also sign in with Google by clicking the "Sign in with Google" button.

![Login Page](/images/login_prod.png)

### Forgot Password

If you forgot your password:
1. Click the "Forgot password?" link on the login page.
2. Enter your email in the dialog that appears.
3. Click "Send Reset Link".
4. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

## Creating an Account

If you don't have an account:
1. Click on the "Sign up" link at the bottom of the login page.
2. Enter your Email or Phone number.
3. Enter a Password and confirm it.
4. Click "Create account".

You can also create an account using Google.

![Signup Page](/images/signup_prod.png)

## Verifying Your Email

After registration, if your email is unconfirmed, you may see a message or restricted access until you verify it. Follow the link sent to your email to complete verification.
