# Groups

This guide covers how to create, join, and manage groups in the Group Mini application.

## Onboarding and Prerequisites

Before you can create or fully participate in groups, you must complete the onboarding journey:
1. **Verify Legal Identity**: You must validate your identity documents securely with Didit.
2. **Account Setup**: Once verified, your account and owner permissions will be set up.
3. **Join or Create Group**: This step is locked until you complete the previous steps.

![Onboarding Journey](/images/dashboard_prod.png)

## Creating a Group

To create a group:
1. Navigate to the "My Groups" section.
2. Click the "Create Group" button.
3. If you haven't verified your identity, a "Verification Required" modal will appear. You must click "Verify Identity" to proceed.

![Verification Required](/images/verification_required_prod.png)

## Group Setup

After creating a group, it enters the "SETUP" state. You must complete a checklist of items before the group can be activated and start its cycle.

![Group Setup](/images/group_setup.png)

The checklist typically includes:
- **Pot per rotation set**: Configure the target pot amount and currency in the **Settings** tab under the Operations section.
- **Cycle defined**: Define the sequence and frequency of collections (e.g., Daily, Weekly) in the **Settings** tab.
- **All members joined**: Fill all member spots before commencing. The member limit is set in the group settings.
- **Start date set**: Select the official start date in the **Schedules** tab.

Click "Resume Setup" to navigate directly to the next incomplete step.

## Managing Groups

When you select a group, you can access several tabs to manage it:
- **Overview**: Summary of group activities.
- **Members**: List of current and pending members.
- **Schedules**: Calendar and timeline of activities.
- **Finances**: Financial logs and settings.
- **Settings**: General group settings.

### Managing Members

In the **Members** tab, you can manage the group's roster.

#### Inviting Members
To invite a member:
1. Click the "Invite member" button.
2. Search for the user by Phone or Email.
3. Select a role for the user.
4. Click "Invite".

Invited users will appear in the list with a "Pending" status until they accept.

![Members Table Pending](/images/members_table_pending.png)

#### Accepting Invitations
If you are invited to a group, you will see a notification or the group will appear in your list. You can view pending invitations in your profile settings (see Profile section) and accept them there.

#### Rejected or Revoked Invitations
- **If you reject an invitation**: It will be removed from your invitations list, and you will not be added to the group.
- **If an admin revokes an invitation**: It will disappear from your list, and the spot will become available for another user.

## Finding Groups

On the main dashboard or "Discover Groups" page, you can search for groups and apply advanced filters to find the right fit.

### Advanced Filters
Click the filters icon to access advanced search criteria:
- **Type**: Filter by group type (Rotation or Accumulation).
- **Status**: Filter by group status (e.g., Active, Setup, Completed).
- **Currency**: Filter by the currency used.
- **Contribution Amount**: Set a minimum contribution amount.
- **Spots Left**: Filter by available spots.
- **Payout Method**: Filter by payout logic (e.g., random, fixed).

![Advanced Filters](/images/advanced_filters.png)

## Group Schedules

In the **Schedules** tab of a group, you can view the timeline of events:
- **Collections**: Scheduled dates for member contributions.
- **Payouts**: Scheduled dates for distribution of funds.
- Events are organized by rounds or cycles.

![Group Schedules](/images/group_schedules.png)

## Group Finances

In the **Finances** tab, you can monitor the financial state of the group:
- **Accounts**: View balances and linked accounts.
- **Logs**: Review transaction history and contribution logs.

![Group Finances](/images/group_finances.png)
