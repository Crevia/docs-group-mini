# Troubleshooting & Edge Cases

This guide covers common edge cases, errors, and how to resolve them.

## Page Not Found (404)
If you see a "Page Not Found" screen, the URL you accessed does not exist. Click "Go to Home" to return to the dashboard.

## Group Not Found
If you try to access a group and see "Group not found", it may be because:
- The group ID is invalid.
- You do not have permission to view the group (e.g., you are not a member of a private group).
- The group has been deleted.

![Group Not Found](/images/group_not_found.png)

## Service Unavailable
If the application shows "Service Unavailable" or fails to fetch data:
- Check your internet connection.
- This might be due to a temporary connection issue or server maintenance. Click "Try Again" to refetch.

## Identity Verification Blocked
If your verification is blocked (e.g., duplicate account detected):
- You cannot proceed with group creation or joining.
- Contact support via the live chat widget to resolve the issue.

## Failed to Join Group
If you cannot join a group, check if:
- The group is already at max capacity.
- The group is active and no longer accepting members.
- You have reached your own account limit for groups.
