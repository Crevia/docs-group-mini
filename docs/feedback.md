# Feedback

We value your input. Your feedback helps us improve Group Mini for everyone.

## How to Share Feedback

You can share feedback in the following ways:

- **Bug reports** — If something isn't working as expected, describe the steps to reproduce it, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened.
- **Feature requests** — If you have an idea for a new feature or improvement, describe the problem it solves and how you envision it working.
- **General feedback** — Share your overall experience, what you like, and what could be better.

## What to Include

Good feedback includes:

- A clear description of the issue or suggestion
- Steps to reproduce (for bugs)
- The device and browser you're using
- Screenshots or recordings where helpful

## Response Times

We review all feedback regularly. For critical bugs affecting account access or financial data, we prioritize resolution. Feature requests are evaluated against current roadmap priorities.

Thank you for helping make Group Mini better.
